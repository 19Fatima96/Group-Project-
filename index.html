About Us

We are a group of girls from East london who study either Mathematics or Medicine at Queen Mary University. What we have in common, is a great passion for sports and fitness. We believe it is essential for females to participate in sports and fitness activities as it is beneficial for them not only on a physical level but also on a psychological and social level.

However we have found that a lot of women in East london are holding themselves back from partaking in such activites as they are not comfortable in the mixed environments in which these activities take place.

We have dedicated this page to locating all the female-only fitness and sports activities taking place in East london so that all women may participate in sporting activities of their choice in an environment they are comfortable in.
